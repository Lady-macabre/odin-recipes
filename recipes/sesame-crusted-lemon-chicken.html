Sesame Crusted Lemon Chicken


Serves 4

What is Sesame Crusted Lemon Chicken

Sesame Crusted Lemon Chicken is a simple Asian dish.



Steps

  1. Put the sesame seeds in a wide bowl or dish and add grated lemon zest to the mixture with a pinch of salt.
  2. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and squeeze in lemon juice.
  3. Pat the chicken dry and dip each breast one at at time in the egg. Let the excess drain off, then coat in the sesame seed and lemon zest mixture.
  4. Heat the oil in a pan to a moderate heat. Place the chicken breasts in the pan and cook until the coating is golden brown and crisp, ensuring the chicken is cooked thoroughly throughout.


Serving Suggestions

Serve alongside a flavourful stir-fry or some rice.
