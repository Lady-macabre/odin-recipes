Lasagne

What is Lasagne?

Lasagne is a type of pasta which is traditionally baked as part of the Italian dish consisting of meat, vegetables and a cheese sauce. Lasagne is often served alongside Garlic Bread.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Lean beef mince
  • Tomato puree
  • Tomatoes, Diced
  • Onion, diced
  • Beef stock
  • Lasagne Sheets
  • White Sauce
  • Mozzarella, grated
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. In a large pan, heat the olive oil on a low heat. Fry the onion until softened, then add the mince and fry on a meduim heat until browned.
  2. Once the meat is evenly browned, stir in the tomato puree, chopped tomatoes and beef stock.
  3. Preheat the oven (gas Mark 6, 200OC conventional oven or 180OC fan oven).
  4. In a baking dish, layer the ingredients starting with the meat and sauce, followed by a layer of lasagne sheets and topped with white sauce. Repeat this step two more times.
  5. Top the lasagne with the grated mozzarella ana Parmesan.
  6. Place in the oven and cook for 45 minutes until crisp and brown on top.
  7. Ensure the lasagne is piping hot throughout before serving.
